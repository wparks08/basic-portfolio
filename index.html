About Me

Will Parks

My name is Will Parks. Originally from a small town in Marin County, I now live in Orangevale, CA with my Wife and (soon to be born!) daughter.

I first started my life-long career with computers at around the age of 4, when I built my first computer (with supervision, of course!). I received my first programming book in C in the 4th grade, and have been hooked ever since. This led me to study Computer Science in college, obtaining a certification in HTML/CSS Web Content Development, and an Associates of Science in Computer Science.
